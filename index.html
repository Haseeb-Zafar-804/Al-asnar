Help is Our Main Goal

Al-Ansar Education & Welfare Association is dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through various initiatives, including educational programs, healthcare services, and distributing essential resources.

Al-Ansar Education & Welfare Association has been working tirelessly to bring positive change to underserved areas. We provide resources like food, clothing, and access to education to help individuals grow and succeed.

Our Projects

Founder Profile

Asif Raza

Asif Raza has been dedicated to helping people for over 10 years, successfully qualifying more than 100,000 students in CIT, full-stack web development, languages, and Taekwondo. His expertise and commitment to education have empowered countless individuals to excel in their careers and personal growth. Asif's leadership and guidance have made a lasting impact on many lives. Through his innovative training methods, he has created a pathway for students to gain real-world skills. His passion for nurturing talent continues to inspire and drive positive change. Asif’s contributions to education are a testament to his dedication and vision for the future.
